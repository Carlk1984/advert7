What Are Backlinks?

Backlinks, also known as inbound links or incoming links, are created when one website links to another. Backlinks are important for SEO because search engines like Google use them as a signal of the quality and relevance of the content.

How Backlinks Work

Search Engine Crawlers: When search engine bots crawl a webpage, they follow the links on that page to discover new content. If a static webpage links to another site, the search engine can follow that link and recognize it as a backlink.

Quality and Relevance: The value of a backlink depends on the quality and relevance of the linking page. High-quality backlinks from authoritative sites are more beneficial for SEO.
